Overview

Oncology is the branch of medicine dedicated to the study, diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of cancer. This field encompasses various approaches to cancer care, including medical oncology (using drugs), radiation oncology (using radiation), and surgical oncology (using surgery). Oncologists lead multidisciplinary teams to provide care for patients from diagnosis through treatment and follow-up
